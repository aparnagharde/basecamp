Online Ordering Settings

How to configure Online Ordering information - like Sales Tax, Tips, confirmation emails & SMS?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021

  • Step 1: Go to "Digital Menu" tab on your PringleAPI Account
  • Step 2: Click on "Online Ordering"
  • Step 3: Click on "General Settings"
  • Step 4: Check the "Support Online Ordering" option to enable the online ordering of the website
  • Step 5: Check the "Accept Tips?" option to accept the Tips
  • Step 6: Check the "Accept Orders When Closed?" option to accept the orders when the restaurant is closed
    • Note: The pick up time for the food is still during the open the hours of the restaurant
  • Step 7: Add Sales Tax depending upon State, County and Local tax
  • Step 8: Customization of Online Order message can be done using this field. Typically it is the best practice to know the customer the wait time of the order
    • Ex: Online Order take 20-30 minutes approximately
  • Step 9: Online Order confirmation emails can be sent to the restaurant owners and managers. Use this field to enter the email address to the recipients
    • Note: Email confirmation includes all the order details
    • Note: Enter multiple separated by "Comma"
    • Ex: john@gmail.com,rj@yahoo.com
  • Step 10: Online Order Confirmation can also be sent to your mobile in the form of SMS
    • Note: SMS confirmation includes only order number of the customer
    • Note: Format is Phonenumber@provider. Provider domains are as listed below
    • Note: Enter multiple separated by "Comma"
    • Ex: 6035844799@tmomail.net,7547547547@txt.att.net
  • Step 11: Click on "Update Online Ordering Setting" at the end of the window to the update the settings
How to configure Online Order Pick up Settings?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021

  • Step 1: Go to "Digital Menu" on your PringleAPI Account
  • Step 2: Click on "Online Ordering"
  • Step 3: Go to "Pickup Settings"
  • Step 4: Set the Time Limit for Online Pickup Order in mins after open of day or current time
    • Ex: 15 indicates taking online pickup orders after 15 minutes from open of day or current time
      • Step 5: Set Time Limit for Online Pickup Order in mins prior to close of day
        • Ex: 15 indicates taking online pickup orders after 15 minutes from open of day or current time
        • Note: When setting the time for tha last order, you have to take into consideration of the time from the current order
        • Ex: Time from current order: 15, Time prior to close of the day:15, so the last order is 30 mins before the closing hours
      • Step 6: Click on "Update Online Ordering Settings" at the end of the window to update the settings
How to configure Online Order Delivery Settings?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021


  • Step 1:Go to "Digital Menu" on your PringleAPI Account
  • Step 2: Click on "OnlineOrdering"
  • Step 3:Go to "Delivery Settings"
  • Step 4:Check the "Enable Delivery" option to enable the delivery of the food by the restaurant
  • Step 5: Check "Apply Delivery Rate Before Tax" to include the delivery rate in the tax
  • Step 6:Select the option on how to calculate the Delivery rate - "Distance" or "Amount"
  • Step 7:Select the option on Delivery range - "Distance" (6a) or "Specific ZipCodes"
    • Step 7(a):Enter the range in miles within which you would deliver
      • Ex:If you would deliver within 6 miles use 6 as the value
    • Step 7(b):Enter the Delivery Zip Codes within which you would deliver
      • Ex:Enter the zipcodes where delivery will be available. This is an optional field. Enter multiple zip codes separated by comma
  • Step 8: Threshold amount - Enter the amount above which delivery charges would be applied.
  • Step 9: Enter Time Limit for Online Delivery Order in mins after open of window or current time
    • Note:15 indicates taking online Delivery orders after 15 minutes from open of window or current time
  • Step 10: Enter Time Limit for Online Delivery Order in mins prior to close of Window
    • Note:15 indicates taking online Delivery orders 15 minutes prior to close of window
  • Step 11:Delivery Order confirmation emails can be sent to the restaurant owners and managers. Use this field to enter the email address to the recipients
    • Note:Email confirmation includes all the order details
    • Note:Enter multiple separated by "Comma"
    • Ex:john@gmail.com,rj@yahoo.com
  • Step 12:Delivery Order Confirmation can also be sent to your mobile in the form of SMS
    • Note:SMS confirmation includes only order number of the customer
    • Note:Format is Phonenumber@provider. Provider domains are as listed below
    • Note:Enter multiple separated by "Comma"
    • Ex:6035844799@tmomail.net,7547547547@txt.att.net
  • Step 13:Delivery window timings can be set. Enter the times during the weekday for the delivery
  • Step 14:Click on "Update Online delivery Settings"

How to turn off/ turn on online ordering?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021


  • Step 1: Go to "Digital Menu" on your PringleAPI Account
  • Step 2: Click on "Online Ordering"
  • Step 3: Click on "General Settings" and check "Support Online Ordering" to turn on Online Ordering and uncheck "Support Online Ordering" to turn off Online Ordering
  • Step 4: Now click on "Update Online Ordering Settings" Button at the bottom
  • Step 5: Open the website to verify."Checkout" option should not be available
How to restrict Online Ordering of products to a certain time only?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021
CLICK HERE TO WATCH THE VIDEO .



  • Step 1:Go to "Digital Menu" tab in your PringleAPI Account
  • Step 2:Click on "Availability Type"
  • Step 3:Enter the "Availability Name"
    • Ex:Lunch Hours, Express Menu
  • Step 4:Click on "Add Availability Type"
  • Step 5:To edit the existing availability type, click on the "Pencil" icon
  • Step 6:Click on "Update Availability Type"
  • Step 7:Go the "Products" under "Digital Menu"
  • Step 8:Select the product for which the availability type has to be setup
  • Step 9:Click on "Manage availability Restriction" next to the product
  • Step 10:Select the weekday and availability time of the day
  • Step 11:Click on "Add availability Restriction"
