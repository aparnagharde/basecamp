Reports & Analytics

How to view sales for a specific time period?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021

  • Step 1: Go to "Digital Menu" on your Pringle API Account
  • Step 2: Click on "Sales Transactions"
  • Step 3: Enter From and To Dates
  • Step 4:Click on "Search" to get the sales details
How to view trend of sales by month, hour or weekday?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021


  • Step 1: Go to "Analytics" on your Pringle API Account
  • Step 2: Click on "Orders"
  • Step 3: To View Monthly Sales - Click on "By Month", Select Year and Click on Get Sales
  • Step 4: To View Hourly Sales - Click on "By Hour", Select Dates and Click on Get Sales By Hour
  • Step 5: To View Weekday Sales - Click on "By Weekday", Select Dates and Click on Get Sales By Weekday
How to view products sales trend by month, hour and weekday?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021

  • Step 1: Go to "Analytics" link on your Pringle API Account
  • Step 2:Click on "Product"
  • Step 3:Click on "Daily Sales"
  • Step 4: Add Dates and click on Search to get Product Sales
How to view Settlement Reports?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021

  • Step 1: Go to "Analytics" link on your Pringle API Account
  • Step 2:Click on "Settlements"
  • Step 3:Click on "Online Orders"
  • Step 4:Select Month and Click on Search to get Settlement Reports
How to view Statements?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021

  • Step 1: Go to "Manage Account" link on your Pringle API Account
  • Step 2: Click on "Statements"
  • Step 3: Select Year and click on individual statement to get details of Invoice.
