Manage Digital Menu

How to update/create a new Menu Category or Sub Category?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021
CLICK HERE TO WATCH VIDEO




  • Step 1: To create new Menu Category, Go to “Digital Menu” on your pringle API account
  • Step 2: In Digital Menu go to “categories”
  • Step 3: Click on the “Add New” icon to create new menu category
  • Step 4: Give the Name for the category to be added
  • Step 5: Enter Tag line for the category. (Tag line is not mandatory)
  • Step 6: Enter the “sort order” number for the new category
  • Step 7: Select the options depending on the requirement
  • Step 8: Click on the “Add Category” to add the new category
  • Note: To create new Subcategory, Follow the same steps. For each Subcategory, a category has to be assigned
  • Note: To update category/subcategory click on the pencil icon, edit the information and “Update”
How to update/create a new Menu Product?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021



  • Step 1: To create new Menu Product go to “Digital Menu” in your PringleAPI account
  • Step 2: Click on the Products
  • Step 3: Now click on “Add New” icon
  • Step 4: Give the name of the product to be added
  • Step 5: Select the “Category” and “Sub Category” to the product
  • Step 6: Description should include the product details
  • Step 7: Enter the "Price" details
  • Step 8: Give the “Sort Order” number for the new product
  • Step 9: Give Quantity of the product available in inventory.
  • Step 10: Select the required fields according to the requirement.
  • Step 11: Select applicable Spice Levels for the particular product.
  • Step 12: Select the “Weekday” of the product availability
  • Step 13: Click on Choose File to add image for a particular product. (Size: 250x250)
  • Step 14: Click on “Add Product” to add new product.

How to enable/disable a category , subcategory (or) product?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021


  • Step 1: Click on the circle icon next to product –if the circle is filled with color, then the product is enabled
  • Step 2: If the circle is not filled with the color, then the product is disabled

How to create Tables with QR Dine In Codes?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021
CLICK HERE TO WATCH THE VIDEO FOR QR DINE IN.


  • Step 1: Go to your PringleAPI account
  • Step 2: Go to Digital Menu
  • Step 3: Go to Tables
  • Step 4: Click on Add Table
  • Step 5: Add Table Name
  • Step 6: Click Add Table. Table will be added.
  • Step 7: Click on Configure Bistro QR Dine In
  • Step 8: Choose level of Automation
  • Step 9: Choose Menu Categories
  • Step 10: Choose if Promo Codes can be used
  • Step 11: Choose if Customer can give special Instructions
  • Step 12: Click on Save QR Dine In Settings.
  • Step 13: QR Code URL will be generated for corresponding table.
  • Step 14: Click on Image icon to generate QR Code Flyer for Dine In.
How to view current live customers on the website?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021

  • Step 1: Go to "Digital Menu" tab on your PringleAPI Account
  • Step 2: Click on "Real Time Carts"
  • Step 3: You see a list of all the visitor carts that are created today in the website
  • Step 4: Click on the eye of a particular customer to see the details
    • Note: If the customers cart shows "Active", the products are added to the cart
    • Note: If the order was placed, "Active" is turned in "Paid"
How to add Custom Spice Level to Category or Product?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021
CLICK HERE TO WATCH THE VIDEO.



  • Step 1: Go to "Digital Menu" tab on your PringleAPI Account
  • Step 2: Click on Custom Spicy Level.
  • Step 3: Enter Custom Spice Name.
  • Step 4:Click On Add Spicy Level.
  • Step 5:All Custom Spicy Levels will be listed here.
  • Step 6:Go to Digital Menu -> Categories.
  • Step 7:Click on Yellow Circle in front of Category for which spice level needs to be added.
  • Step 8:Select Spice levels. (You can multiple usint Ctrl Key)
  • Step 9:Click on Update Spice level.
How to update inventory for a product?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021

  • Step 1: Go to "Digital Menu" tab on your PringleAPI Account
  • Step 2: Click on Products and select edit for the particular product.
  • Step 3: Add the quantity in inventory for particular item.
  • Step 4: Select the checkbox "Track Quantity of item in inventory". Hit Update.
