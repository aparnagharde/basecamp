Sales Order Management

How to view online Orders for specified date range?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021

  • Step 1: Go to "Digital Menu" tab on your PringleAPI Account
  • Step 2: Click on "Sales Transactions"
  • Step 3: Enter the Start and End date
    • Note: By default, the screen shows today’s transaction
  • Step 4: You can Search for a particular transaction based on the Customer Name, Customer Order, and Transaction Price
How to refund an Online Order?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021
CLICK HERE TO WATCH THE VIDEO .


  • Step 1: Go to "Digital Menu " tab on your PringleAPI Account
  • Step 2: Click on "Sales Transactions"
  • Step 3: Enter the Start and End date
    • Note: If you are searching based on customer’s credit card transaction date, select a range two days prior and two days after. This is because it might take minimum of one day for the transaction to be settled
  • Step 4: Confirm the Customer by his Name, Date, Transaction Price
  • Step 5: After selecting the Customer, click on the "Refund" Icon next to his Name
  • Step 6 & 7: Enter the Amount in the Dialog box and click Refund
    • Note: For partial refunds, please wait one business day
  • Step 8: After the amount was successfully refunded you will see the status as "Completed"
    • Note: It can take up to 3 business days for the charge to be reflected in the end customer banking statement
    • If refunded within 24 hours of initial order time, the transaction will be voided and the customer will not be charged. In this scenario, the customer’s banking statement will not show as refund
How to reprint an existing order?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021

  • Step 1: Go to "Digital Menu" tab on your PringleAPI Account
  • Step 2: Click on "Sales Transactions"
  • Step 3: Select the Customer for whose order has to be reprinted
  • Step 4: Click on the "Read" Icon (third in the list) next to the selected Customer Name
    • Note: Circle will be fully colored. After one minute, the order will be printed in the kitchen
How to check disputes raised by the customer?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021

  • Step 1: Go to "Digital Menu" tab on your PringleAPI Account
  • Step 2: Click on "Disputes"
  • Step 3: You see all disputes categorized as Pending, Won, Lost.
