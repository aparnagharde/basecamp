eAgent - IVR & Phone Orders

How to review phone calls logs for IVR?

  • Step 1: Go to "eAgent" tab on your PringleAPI Account
  • Step 2: Click on "Call Log"
  • Step 3: Enter the Start and End date
How to review orders taken via eAgent?

  • Step 1:Go to "Digital Menu" tab on your PringleAPI Account
  • Step 2: Click on "Sales Transactions"
  • Step 3: Enter the Start and End date
  • Note: By default, the screen shows today’s transaction
  • Step 4: To filter for only eAgent transactions - please enter eAgent into the search box.
  • Note: Use this link for a mobile friendly version of Sales Transactions Screen. https://pringleapi.com/MyAccount/M_ManageDigitalMenuSales
How to take payment in store for eAgent Orders?



  • Step 1:: Navigate to the mobile friendly Sales Transaction Page by visiting: https://pringleapi.com/MyAccount/M_ManageDigitalMenuSales
  • Step 2: Identify the transaction you want to take payment for.
  • Step 3a: To take CC payment click on the "$" icon next the above transaction. Enter the CC payment details and click on "Accept Payment" button.
  • Step 3b: To take cash payment or process payment via another offline in-store method, click on the "Cash Drawer" icon. Then click on the "Yes - Accept Payment (Offline)" button.
