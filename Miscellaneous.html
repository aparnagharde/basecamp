How to place an Order with Franchise Central Kitchen?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021


  • Step 1: Go to "Central Kitchen" on your Pringle API Account
  • Step 2: Go to "Place Orders"
  • Step 3: Select the Category
  • Step 4: Add the Items to the cart
  • Step 5: Proceed for Checkout
  • Step 6: To change the quantity, enter a number in the Quantity box and click Update. To remove an item from your Shopping Cart, click Delete
  • Step 7: Enter Name, Email, Phone Number, and shipping address
  • Step 8: Click "Place Order"
  • Step 9: A Corporate Representative will contact with next steps.
How to provide BistroStack Delegate Access to GoDaddy Account?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021

  • Step 1: Login to your GoDaddy Account
  • Step 2: Go to "Account Settings"
  • Step 3: Click "Delegate Access"
  • Step 4: Click "Grant Access"
  • Note: You can give and manage the Access Levels. Pringle doesn't need the account passwords. Give Pringle the Delegate Access and we will host the Website
How to provide BistroStack admin Access to Facebook?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021

  • Step 1: Login to FaceBook with the Business Credentials
  • Step 2: Go to Settings
  • Step 3: Click "Page Roles"
  • Step 4: Provide Email address in the Dialog box
  • Note: The Email address should be associated with the FaceBook
  • Note: Provide the FaceBook access to studios@pringlesoft.com
  • Step 5: Assign the Page Role
  • Note: Pringle needs "Admin" access to effectively manage Facebook Business Page
  • Step 6: Click "Add" Button
  • Step 7: An Invitation email will be sent to Pringle
How to Provide Bistrostack admin access to Google Business?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021

  • Step 1: Go to "business.google.com"
  • Step 2: Login with your Google Business account
  • Step 3: Click on the Bar it will redirect you to New Window with all the Location Details and Amenities
  • Step 4: Click on "Who Has Access"
  • Step 5: Opens a New Window to Manage Permissions - Click on + Icon on the Right Corner of the Window
  • Step 6: Enter the Email address and assign the Role
  • Step 7: When Completed Click on Done
  • Note: An Invitation will be sent to the provided Email address, which has to be accepted to Edit the Details of the Business
  • Provide Google Business Access to "pringlesocial@gmail.com"
