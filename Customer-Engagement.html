How to publish a News Article or Blog online?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021


  • Step 1: Go to "News" on your PringleAPI Account
  • Step 2: Click on "Add New"
  • Step 3: Provide the Appropriate Headline
  • Step 4: Select the Date
  • Step 5: Add the content in the body
  • Step 6: if you want to add the image, Click on the Image Icon. A new windows will pop-up
  • Step 7: Provide the URL of the Flyer
    • Note: The Flyer must be uploaded into documents. Once the flyer is uploaded, right click on the flyer and copy of the URL
    • Note: For better SEO performance, use appropriate naming convention for the images
      • EX: NameofRestaurant_Nameoftheimage
        (Bistrostack_Fathers_Day)
  • Step 8: Click on the padlock. It will unlock.
  • Step 9: Provide the Width as "100%" and Height as to be Blank
  • Step 10: Click on "OK"
  • Step11: Now Click on "Add News" at the Bottom of the page

How to send a Mass SMS to customers?

CLICK HERE TO WATCH THE VIDEO .

  • Step 2b: Enter Custom Recepients if any.

How to create a discount coupon on the website?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021
CLICK HERE TO WATCH THE VIDEO .




  • Step 1: Go to "Promotions" on your PringleAPI Account
  • Step 2: Click on "Codes"
  • Step 3: Click on "Add Promotion Code"
  • Step 4: Create the name of Promotion Code.
  • Step 5: Add all details related to promo code.
    • Note: Promotion Codes can be generated for Discount Amount or Discount Percentage
    • If Discount amount is given, Enter the Discount Percentage as 0 and vice versa
    • Under the Threshold amount - Offer will be utilized only if the customer purchases above the amount (Optional)
  • Enter the Start and End Date of the Offer
  • Select the product option only if there is an offer on a selected product
  • Select the Customer option if you want to provide to a certain customer
  • Enter the quantity limit if required
  • Step 6: Check the Boxes as per your requirements
  • Step 7: Select promotion code applicable days and timings.
  • Step 8: Click on "Add Promotion Code" at the bottom of the page

Add to Calendar Configuration
Content
  • Format: {Add to Calendar}&start={starttime}&end={endtime}&title={title}&description={description}&timezone={timezone}&service={service}
  • Service values are apple, google, office365, outlook, outlookcom and yahoo
  • Timezone - Click Here to identify the Timezone
  • Add To Calendar - Google
    • Example:{Add to Calendar}&start=10/26/2020 08:00 AM&end=10/26/2020 10:00 AM&title=Summary of the event&description=Description of the Event
      Next Line&timezone=America/Los_Angeles&service=google
  • Add To Calendar - Apple
    • Example:{Add to Calendar}&start=10/26/2020 08:00 AM&end=10/26/2020 10:00 AM&title=Summary of the event&description=Description of the Event
      Next Line&timezone=America/Los_Angeles&service=apple
  • Add To Calendar - Outlook

      Next Line&timezone=America/Los_Angeles&service=outlook
  • Add To Calendar - Outlook.com

System Variables Usage:
The following variables are reserved and can be used to construct the communication subject and content
  • {Business Name} - will be replaced by your Business Name as configured in the Profile.
    SubjectContent
  • {Form Name} - will be replaced by the name of this form
    SubjectContent
  • {Customer} - will be replaced by the firstname and lastname of the submitter
    SubjectContent
  • {Tabular Form Data} - will be replaced by the tabular data of the form
    Content
Example Subject
  • Event Registrations
    {Business Name}: Congratulations! Now you are registered for {Form Name}
  • Form Submissions
    {Business Name}: Thank you for Submitting {Form Name}

  • Form Submissions
