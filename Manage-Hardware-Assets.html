How to troubleshoot if your Online Ordering Device is down?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021



  • Note:Steps to troubleshoot "KangaroBOX"
  • Step 1: Check power supply is connected properly or not, Check if the lights are ON
  • Step 2: Check if the Ethernet wire light is blinking or Not, if its not blinking unplug the cable and Plug-in again
  • Step 3: Blue light states that the device is ON and orange light indicates the device is getting charged.
  • Step 4: Check if the dock is kept in a right way or not.
  • Step 5: If above all steps in right way – please restart the device "Long Press Power button 5-10sec device is OFF then again Long Press Power button 5-10sec device is ON "
How to troubleshoot if your Online Ordering Tablet is down?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021

  • Step 1: Make sure that tablet is fully charged, If not put in charge
  • Step 2: Check WiFi connection.
  • Step 3: If both network & power are ON – restart the Tablet.
How to troubleshoot printing issues?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021





  • Step 1:Go to "Setting >> Printer & Scanner " Check printer status printer are Offline or Online, If in case offline check Online Order device IP address and Printer Port IP address both are connected in same Network
  • Step 2: Turn your receipt printer off and unplug all cables. Plug all cables back in and turn the receipt printer on. Make sure the receipt printer is fully plugged in and a roll of paper is in the printer. Make sure that your Ethernet cable is connected to the right piece of hardware. You may need to switch the direction of the cable.
  • Step 3: Open COMMAND PROMPT and Type Ipconfig >> Enter then shows IP ADDRESS and Default GateWay and Subnet mask.
  • Step 4: To get list of connected devices Type "arp -a" >> Enter
  • Step 5: To check the status of the device Type "Ping" Space Device IP address Enter
  • Step 6: Check Log any error accord or not(Go to MyPC >> C: Drive >> PringleAPI >> ServiceLogs >> OnlineOrderPrinter.Log)
  • Step 7: Open Task Scheduler Expand Task Scheduler Library then goto PringleAPI check all files each and every step once complete RUN all files Under PringleAPI
  • Step 8: Open Event Viewer check any error message notify or Not
