Manage Your Account

How to update your profile info?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021

  • Step 1: Go to "Manage Account" on your PringleAPI Account
  • Step 2: Click on "Profile"
  • Step 3: Enter/update your details
  • Step 4: Now click on "Update Profile" Button at the bottom
How to add additional users to your account and assign a role?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021

  • Step 1: Go to "Manage Account" on your PringleAPI Account
  • Step 2: Click on "Users"
  • Step 3: To add New User - Click on "Add New" button
  • Step 4: Now enter a User Name, Password and select the modules the users should have access to. Once done click on "Add User" button
How to update and existing user?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021

  • Step 1: Go to "Manage Account" on your PringleAPI Account
  • Step 2: Click on "Users"
  • Step 3: Click on "Pencil" icon next to the User you want to update
  • Step 4: Now update user information like Password or Modules. Once done click on "Update User" button
How to view Statements ?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021

  • Step 1: Go to Pringle API -> Manage Account.
  • Step 2: Go to Statements.
  • Step 3: Click on Year for which you want to view statement.
  • Step 4: Click on the document to see the statement.
How to add custom message on stanadard emails sent while placing order?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021

  • Step 1: Go to Pringle API -> Manage Account.
  • Step 2: Go to Communication Settings.
  • Step 3: Click on Order Confirmation.
  • Step 4: Add the custom message (Text or Image).
How to add custom message for Feedback emails?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021

  • Step 1: Go to Pringle API -> Manage Account.
  • Step 2: Go to Communication Settings.
  • Step 3: Click on Feedback Request.
  • Step 4: Add the custom message (Text or Image).
