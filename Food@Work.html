How to create/update restricted menu for Food@Work or Catering Pages?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021


  • Step 1: Go to "Digital Menu" on your PringleAPI Account
  • Step 2: Click on "Categories"
  • Step 3: For Food@Work, create a new category (Ex: Lunch Boxes) and then click on the pencil icon next to the category name
  • Step 4: Get the “id=xxxxx” numerical value from the URL of the page
    • Note: Just the numerical value has to be copied
    • Ex: Copy 29923 from the URL “
      https://www.pringleapi.com/MyAccount/
      AddEditMenuCategory?id=29923”
  • Step 5: Go to "Key Value Pairs" Tab
  • Step 6: Enter the KeyValuePair "RestrictedMenu1"
  • Step 7: Enter the code copied in step 4 in the variable value field. Multiple categories can be added separated by coma
    • Ex: 233347,288879,333333
  • Step 8: Click on "Add Key Value Pair"
  • Step 9: Add "FoodAtWorkURL" in the Variable Key field
  • Step 10: Select the menu template URL from the "Information" under "Digital Menu" tab in your PringleAPI account
  • Step 11: Remove cartesse section from the URL and Add
    &restrictedmenu=1&promos=1&offlineship=1&FoodAtWork=1
    • Ex: https://www.pringleapi.com/PringleDigitalMenuClient/
      Template1?token=cbde398a-bcdef-40a8-8bcb-47fc77c86468&&restrictedmenu=1
      &promos=1&offlineship=1&FoodAtWork=1
  • Step 12: Click on "Add Key Value Pair"
  • Step 13: Follow the same steps for Mobile menu. Add “FoodAtWorkURLMobile” in the Variable Key Field. Follow the steps 10 to 12
  • Step 14: To create a Delivery menu, follow the same steps to add "RestrictedMenu2"
    • Note: If the menu for Food@Work and Delivery2Community are same, skip the steps 1- 8
  • Step 15: In "Key Value Pairs" tab, Add Delivery2CommunityURL and CommunityDeliveryUrlMobile
  • Step 16: Click on "Add Key Value Pair"
How to create/update Drop Off Locations for Food@Work?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021


  • Step 1: Go to "Food@Work" on your PringleAPI Account
  • Step 2: Click on "Drop Off Points"
  • Step 3: Click "Add New" to add a new location
  • Step 4: Enter the Name of the Drop Off Location
  • Step 5: Enter the Address of the Location
    • Note: Don’t enter name of the city and postal code in the Address line 1
  • Step 6: Enter the Postal Code
  • Step 7: Select weekdays for the delivery of food to the location
  •  
  • Step 8: Select the Cut off time for the location
  • Step 9: Select the Cut off day for the location
  • Step 10: Enter the number in the "#of Pre-order Days"
    • Note: Customer can preorder the food upto 14 days ahead of time. This can be restricted using this field
  • Step 11: Click on "Add Drop Off Point"
  • Step 11: To update the existing drop off location, click on the pencil icon and make necessary changes
  • Step 12: Click on "Update Drop off location"

How to view Food@Work or Delivery2Community Online Orders?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021

  • Step 1: Go to "Food@Work" Tab on your PringleAPI Account
  • Step 2: Click on "Orders Report"
  • Step 3: Select the Date Range
  • Step 4: Select "All Drop Off Points"
  • Step 5: Click on "Search"
How to turn off/ turn on Food@Work & Delivery2Community?
Last Updated: Apr 6, 2021

  • Step 1: Go to "Food@Work" Tab on your PringleAPI Account
  • Step 2: Click on "Drop Off Point"
  • Step 3: Click on Pencil icon for the location to edit
  • Step 4: Uncheck the boxes of weekday for which it was Enabled
  • Step 5: Click on "Update Drop Off Point"
